ADHESIVE & SEALANTS


Loctite and Teroson are Henkel’s flagship brands, leading the world in adhesives, sealants, surface treatments, and more. Loctite, renowned for its breakthrough technology, offers solutions that provide durable, strong, and long-lasting adhesives, unlocking the limitless potential of man and machine. Teroson drives innovation in bonding, sealing, coating, acoustic, stiffening, and reinforcing solutions across OEM transportation assembly, vehicle repair and maintenance (VRM), industrial assemblies, and building industry applications.
